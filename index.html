German Shepherds

Trait Level
Energy High

More info on GSD

German Shepherds are extremely popular dogs in America. They are the second most owned breed according to the AKC. They are common family dogs and have been popular ever since WWII. The most common health problem that these dogs have is hip dysplasia. This is fairly common and should be a concern when searching breeders.

Rottweilers

Trait Level
Energy Mid
Affection High
Intelligence Mid
More info on Rottweilers

Rottweilers are commonly misunderstood dogs. Popular media has often made Rottweilers appear very mean and agressive, but this is not the whole truth. They can be very loving and goofy dogs who are gentle. They love their families and have almost unparalleled loyalty. Rottweilers are famous for having the strongest bite force of any dog in the world.

Belgian Malinois

this is my favorite :)

Trait Level
Energy Very High
Affection Mid
Intelligence High
More info on Belgian Malinois

Belgian malinois are some of the most high energy dogs in the world. Because of this, they are currently the most common military working dog. They make up nearly all of the dogs that are members of U.S. Special Operations Command. These dogs do not make great family pets because of their high energy. If someone wanted to personally own one of these dogs then they should have an extremly active lifestyle and plenty of time to exercise the dog.

Guarding

  1. Rottweiler
  2. German Shepherd
  3. Belgian Malinois
Why Rottweilers win

Rottweilers do not have many separation anxiety issues. They are good dogs to leave overnight as they are very protective of their area, and they do not mind being alone for an extended period of time. They will also bark at nearly anything that moves.

Companionship

  1. Rottweiler & German Shepherd
  2. Belgian Malinois
Why Rottweilers and German Shepherds both win

Rottweilers and German Shepherds are both great dogs to have for yourself. Both are extremely loyal and do great in one-on-one relationships with a single owner. Their energy levels are not too high that they cannot be controlled, but they are high enough to make great exercise partners. They are also big and great for cuddling generally. Rottweilers are generally more affectionate than German Shepherds.

Law-Enforcement/Military Work

  1. Belgian Malinois
  2. German Shepherd
  3. Rottweiler
Why Belgian Malinois win

Belgian Malinois' extremely high energy allows them to be among the best working dogs in the world. They make up nearly all of the dogs used in our armed forces.They are incredibly intelligent which meshes well with their energy levels. A Belgian Malinois recently chased a vital ISIS leader into a cave where he was later killed.

Rottweilers are gentle giants with their families. They often have very goofy and happy personalities when interacting with their families. Additionally, their patience with kids is very high and don't mind being climbed and slept all over.
